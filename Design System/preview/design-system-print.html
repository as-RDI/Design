Wordmark

Logo

The RDI wordmark is the primary brand identifier. Use the dark variant on backgrounds lighter than #CED3DA; use the reversed variant on backgrounds darker than #545B64. Never place the wordmark on the brand red.

02Foundations · Brand MarkP.04
Glyph

Iris Aperture

The iris glyph is reserved for app icons, hero moments, and lockups. Never use it as an inline UI icon — its visual weight is too strong for a button or menu item.

Iris Aperture

Reserved for logo lockups, app icons, hero plates, and splash moments. The glyph echoes the camera aperture that underlies Motion Amplification® — every pixel becomes a sensor.

Minimum rendered size: 24px. Never outline, rotate, or recolor.
